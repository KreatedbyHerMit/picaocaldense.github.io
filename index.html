Welcome to Picao Caldense!

Authentic homemade Colombian salsa piqué — crafted with family, flavor, and heart in Laval, Quebec. This vibrant Colombian sauce is more than just a condiment — it’s a cherished family tradition passed down through generations and lovingly made right here in Laval. Bursting with the bold, fresh flavors of tangy lime, fragrant cilantro, and just the right kick of spice, every bottle brings the warmth and joy of Colombian gatherings to your table.

Whether you drizzle it on empanadas, mix it into your rice, or add a dash to your soup, Picao Caldense brings a taste of home — where culture, passion, and community meet. From our kitchen to yours, every bottle tells a story of love and tradition.
