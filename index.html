Authentic Colombian Salsa

Picao Caldense is a family-owned brand making authentic Colombian salsa piqué in Laval, Quebec. Every bottle is crafted with fresh ingredients, love, and care — bringing the true flavors of Colombia to your table.

Our salsa bursts with the vibrant taste of tangy lime, fresh cilantro, and just the right kick of heat, perfect for enhancing any dish. Whether you drizzle it on empanadas, mix it into rice, or add a dash to soups and stews, each bottle brings warmth, joy, and tradition to your meals.

Beyond flavor, Picao Caldense is a story — of family, culture, and passion. Every batch connects you to generations of Colombian culinary heritage, making every meal an experience to savor. From our kitchen in Laval to your table, we deliver more than salsa — we deliver a piece of Colombia in every bottle.
