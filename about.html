Our Story

I’m Jessica Betancur, founder of Picao Caldense. Growing up in a Colombian family, I was immersed in rich culinary traditions that inspired the creation of our authentic salsa piqué.

Each bottle is handmade with fresh ingredients and authentic Colombian methods, bringing vibrant flavors and culture to your kitchen.

This isn’t just a salsa — it’s a connection between generations, cultures, and communities.
