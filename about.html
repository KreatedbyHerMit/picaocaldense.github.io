About

I’m Jessica Betancur, the proud founder of Picao Caldense — where every bottle of salsa piqué tells a story. Born into a Colombian family with deep culinary roots, I grew up surrounded by the bold flavors, colors, and aromas of our culture. What began as a family recipe has become a symbol of connection, love, and heritage — now bottled for you to enjoy.

Each bottle of Picao Caldense Salsa Piqué is handcrafted in Laval, Quebec, using fresh, local ingredients and traditional Colombian methods. From the tang of lime to the warmth of fresh peppers, every drop brings the spirit of Colombia right to your table.
