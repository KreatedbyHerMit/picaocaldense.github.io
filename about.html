About Picao Caldense

🍽️ 🇨🇴 ❤️

Picao Caldense was born from a passion for authentic Colombian flavors and the warmth of family gatherings. Every dish we serve carries a story — inspired by the culinary traditions passed down through generations.

Based in Laval, we take pride in preparing homemade meals using the finest ingredients and traditional techniques. From our kitchen to your table, we invite you to experience the heart and soul of Colombian comfort food.

Thank you for supporting a local business built on love, culture, and community. ¡Buen provecho!
