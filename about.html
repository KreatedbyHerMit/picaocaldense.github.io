About Us

Welcome to Picao Caldense! I’m Jessica Betancur, the proud founder and president of Picao Caldense. Born into a family rich with Colombian heritage, I grew up surrounded by the aromas and flavors that define our culture. From my family’s kitchen to yours, this journey has been one of love, pride, and authenticity.

Every jar of our salsa piqué is crafted in Laval with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and a deep respect for tradition. Picao Caldense isn’t just a product — it’s a celebration of culture and connection, a way to share a piece of Colombia’s heart with Canada and beyond.
