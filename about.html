Our Story

I’m Jessica Betancur, the proud founder of Picao Caldense. Growing up in a Colombian family with deep culinary roots, I was surrounded by the vibrant colors and flavors that defined our traditions. What began as a simple family recipe for salsa piqué evolved into a passionate mission — to share a taste of Colombia with every table across Canada.

Each bottle of Picao Caldense Salsa Piqué is made by hand with fresh, local ingredients and authentic Colombian methods. From the bright zest of lime to the warmth of fresh peppers, every drop brings the heart of our culture to your kitchen.

This isn’t just a salsa — it’s a legacy. A connection between generations, cultures, and communities. Welcome to the flavor, warmth, and heart of Colombia — welcome to Picao Caldense.
