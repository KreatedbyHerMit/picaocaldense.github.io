About Us

I’m Jessica Betancur, the proud founder of Picao Caldense. Growing up in a Colombian family, I learned the art of crafting salsa piqué from my parents, surrounded by the vibrant flavors and traditions of our culture. Today, every bottle we make in Laval reflects that heritage and passion.

Each bottle is handcrafted with fresh, local ingredients. From the tang of lime to the heat of fresh peppers, every drop carries the warmth and spirit of Colombian cuisine.

Picao Caldense is more than just salsa — it’s a story, a tradition, and a connection to family and culture. Taste a piece of Colombia in every bottle.
