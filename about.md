# About Picao Caldense

Growing up as a first-generation Canadian, my life has been a beautiful blend of two worlds. Though I was born and raised in Canada, my heart has always been deeply connected to Colombia — my country of heritage. Embracing my Colombian roots has been my way of honoring my family’s story and culture.

At Picao Caldense, family, passion, and flavor are at the heart of everything we do. Each bottle of our salsa piqué is a celebration of our Colombian heritage and the joy of sharing good food with loved ones.
