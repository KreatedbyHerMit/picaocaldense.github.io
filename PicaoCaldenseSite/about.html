About Jessica Betancur

Hi, I’m Jessica Betancur, President of Picao Caldense. Born into a Colombian family passionate about food, I continue the tradition of crafting authentic homemade salsa piqué with care and love. Every bottle represents our family’s heritage and dedication to quality. Our Montreal-based kitchen uses only fresh ingredients, ensuring every bottle delivers bold flavors and a story of culture, warmth, and passion.
